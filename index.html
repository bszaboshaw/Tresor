Website for Architecture Practice Canaway Fleming

Website design and build for Canaway Fleming Architects. The practice was established in 2004 and after a very successful few years they wanted to update their online identity. We kept the interface clean and simple to enhance their portfolio. The site aims to highlight their concept driven working process and to illustrate their ability to successfully deliver projects from unique private residences to complex large-scale infrastructure projects.

The site is fully responsive and we also implemented a robust Content Management System.
