Claire Morgan Lifestyle & Interior Stylist

Custom logo and website design for Claire Morgan - one of the leading interior stylists in the UK. The aim was to bring her distinctive philosophy and style to the online identity whilst devising a user interface that will let each piece of work to be viewed without distractions.

The printed and digital identity sits within a well executed digital marketing. The project was a roaring success reaching business targets that the strategy set out to achieve. (Stationery Photography to follow.)
